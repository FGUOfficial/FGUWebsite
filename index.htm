About Us

We are a gaming guild for OGAT, Payday 2, Elder Scrolls Online, Terraria and Omniwar, founded in March 2014. Starting from a small group of friends on Mibbit, we first moved to Skype and later on to Discord where we have had a working and active server for more than a year already. We are more than just a place to play games together - our goal is to create long lasting friendships where people will be there for each other, a place for if someone needs help, advice or just wants to share his/her happiness with others.
For more information about our leaders and moderators check moderation page. If you are interested in our members, check the rules page which also have our members listed, want to see our rules? check the rules page, and if you want to know more about the games we play, go to our games page. If you have issues with the website or just general questions, please contact us on Discord (entity.Hydra(x)#2236 - website designer or Idulus#2479 - guild master). Feel free to take a look at our website and visit us on Discord if you want to join. A huge thank you goes to Hydra, who designed and programmed this website!
