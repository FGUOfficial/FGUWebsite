Games?

While the guild started with "Of Guards and Thieves" (short: OGAT), an indie stealth top-down shooter, we also play "Payday 2", a stealth heist game and "The Elder Scrolls Online" (short ESO), a fantasy MMORPG based on the world of Nirn, Terraria, a 2D Minecraft style game and Omniwar, a sci-fi based 3D first person shooter game. We are highly active in all our games and try to organize events for every game we play. None of our members is required to play all the games, we are fine if someone plays just one or two of them. We also occasionally play other games if enough members are interested, these include minigames such as PnP, Werewolf, Skribbl, etc.
