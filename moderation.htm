About Our Moderation

The guild master, co-leaders, techni-chans and officers are the adminstrators and moderators of the guild. They are responsible for running the guild, organizing events, moderating the Discord server and making sure everyone is having fun. Below you'll find a brief overview:

The Ranks

Guild Master

The guild master is responsible for the whole guild. He has the final vote in difficult decisions and makes sure the guild is running steadily and staying healthy.

Co-Leaders

The co-leaders are responsible for pretty much all important decisions concerning the guild. They are working together with the guild master to decide about the most important changes and work with the officers to help with moderation and daily decisions.

Advisor

The advisor is responsible in assisting the co-leaders and the guild master in case they can't come to a decison. The advisor also gives feedback to the guild master in order to inform him how his decisions are received in the guild and where he can improve the guilds social aspects.

Techni-Chan

The techni-chans are responsible for all technical aspects of the guild. They maintain the website, help with technical issues on the Discord server or with the Discord bots and are always up to answer any tech-related question a member might have.

Officers

The officers are responsible for moderating the Discord server. They make sure, that everyone keeps to the rules, but will also vote on changes, discuss with the admins and help to organize events.
